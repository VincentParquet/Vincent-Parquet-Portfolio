CLIMATE AND BIODIVERSITY CENTER
TYPE   Public building
AREA   4 860 m2
The project of a Climate and Biodiversity Center is based on research - new knowledge -(research laboratories, media library…) and sharing knowledge (museum, lecture hall, meeting room…).

The program focuses on the notion of ecosystem. It not only examines living organisms but it examines the relation between a community of living organisms ( biocenosis ) and its environment (biotope). Consequently, the exhibition spaces are designed as focal (tools) which allow to observe an environment (object).

The Center is situated along the Loire. There is a direct relation between the shape of the building and the nature of the soil. In section, the building is the expression of the nature of the soil (rocky in the south and sandy in the North). The building is therefore constituted by a series of gantry sitting on a wall at the level of the street. Every gantry allows to develop an appropriate tool for the observation of an environment.

Address : Rue du Général Leclerc, 44400 Rezé, FRANCE
