LE PARCOURS DE l'ERDRE
TYPE   Public building
AREA   1 825 m2
The footbridge is aimed at connecting La Chapelle-sur-Erdre with the Chantrerie university center by crossing a remarkable site for its cultural and natural heritage. The bridge uses the structural elements of the arched bridges of l’Erdre (pont général de la Motte-Rouge, pont de la Tortière, pont de la Jonelière…), and tries to be part of its history.

The current crossings do not allow to observe the structure situated under the deck when one cross them. Arcs were voluntarily turned to be able to see this structure. This allows to offer two headlands, at the level of elbows, offering a view on the Castle Nay and to ensure the lateral stability of the structure. Arcs were drawn so as to leave the view on the Castle Nay since the water.

Address : La Chapelle-sur-Erdre (44240) - Carquefou (44470)
